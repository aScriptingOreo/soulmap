Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Soulmap ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website at https://dev.soulmap.7thseraph.org (the "Service").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use the Service, such as custom markers or locations you create.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you access or use the Service, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with the Service, such as the pages or content you view, locations you click on, searches you conduct, and other actions on the Service.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the Service, including the hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Log Information: We collect standard server logs, which include your IP address, browser type and language, access times, and referring website addresses.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activities over time and across different websites. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us improve the Service and your experience, see which areas of the Service are popular, and count visits.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect and process certain analytics data. Google Analytics uses cookies to help us analyze how users use the Site and enhance your experience when you use the Site. Google's ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics about your visits to the Site is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service;
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with the Service;
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent security incidents and other malicious, deceptive, fraudulent, or illegal activity;
  • Debug to identify and repair errors in the Service;
  • Develop new features, products, and services.

Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track ("DNT") signals. If your browser sends a DNT signal, we will not collect analytics data during your visit.

Your Choices

You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

You can also disable analytics collection by adding ?noAnalytics=true to the URL when visiting the site.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of the policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Discord server.
